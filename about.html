About this tool

This site is a simple, independent calculator that estimates Canada Dental Care Plan (CDCP) eligibility and the likely co‑pay bracket (0% / 40% / 60%) based on the inputs you provide.

It does not submit applications, store your data, or contact you. It runs entirely in your browser.

Disclaimers:
This is an independent mathematical estimation tool, not affiliated with the Government of Canada.
Results are estimates only. Final eligibility is determined by Health Canada and CRA.
This is not medical or financial advice.

For official program details, always confirm directly on Government of Canada sources (e.g., Canada.ca).
