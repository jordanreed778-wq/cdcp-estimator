Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 15, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy describes how the CDCP Eligibility Estimator ("the tool," "we," "us") handles information when you visit this website. We are committed to protecting your privacy. In short: this tool does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information you enter.

Information You Enter

When you use the eligibility estimator, you are asked to provide:

  • Your age (in years)
  • Your adjusted family net income
  • Whether you have access to private dental insurance

All calculations are performed entirely within your browser. This information is never sent to any server, never stored in a database, and is deleted as soon as you close or refresh the page. We have no access to it.

Cookies and Tracking

This tool does not use cookies, analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or any form of persistent tracking. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or similar services.

Third-Party Services

This website loads resources from the following third-party CDN providers to render the page correctly:

  • Tailwind CSS CDN (cdn.tailwindcss.com) — for page styling
  • Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) — for the Inter typeface

These services may log your IP address and browser information as part of standard CDN operation, subject to their own privacy policies. We do not control or receive this data. If you prefer not to load these external resources, you can disable them using a browser extension or privacy tool without affecting the estimator logic.

Server Logs

As with any website, your web hosting provider may retain standard server access logs, which typically include your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring page, and the pages you visited. These logs are used for security and operational purposes only and are not shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

This tool is intended for adults seeking information about the Canada Dental Care Plan. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes to the tool or applicable laws. Updates will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the tool following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
